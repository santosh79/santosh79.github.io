Git & Bundler's Gemfile.lock

Git and Bundler are both extremely valuable and heavily used tools in the Ruby commmunity. There are times however when, you are in the middle of the rebase and are really not sure what to do when you encounter a merge conflict in your Gemfile.lock file. How do you go about resolving merge conflicts in this gem manifest that Bundler created for you?

Solution

After your rebase, delete the Gemfile.lock file, eye ball the Gemfile and ensure you have all the gems you need. Then re-run bundle and check in this updated Gemfile.lock file that bundler creates for you.
